About Me

Filmmaker turned makeup artist, I have always been inspired by color. The way colors are able to blend together, amalgamating and metamorhposing as they move across a canvas has always captivated me. For me, makeup is simply a form of art captured on the skin for a fleeting moment in time before being washed away forever. I love the flowing and everchanging form that makeup is able to take. I love the whimsy and mystical qualities it holds. It is everchanging in the world but always constant in my life.
